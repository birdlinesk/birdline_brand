## STEP-BY-STEP UPLOAD PROCESS

### On Canvas.dev:

1. **Go to:** canvas.dev/design-systems
2. **Click:** "Create new design system" or "Set up your design system"
3. **Fill Company Info:**
   - Copy text from STEP 1 above
   - Paste into "Company name and blurb"

4. **Upload Assets:**
   - Go to "Add fonts, logos and assets"
   - Drag all 5 files listed in STEP 2
   - Wait for upload confirmation
   - All files should show as ✓ uploaded

5. **Add Notes:**
   - Scroll to "Any other notes?"
   - Paste full text from STEP 3
   - This preserves all brand guidelines in Canvas

6. **Submit/Finalize:**
   - Review all entries
   - Click "Create Design System" or "Save"
